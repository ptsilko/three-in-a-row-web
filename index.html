Three in a Row

How to Play

Objective: Match 3 or more circles of the same color in a row!

Controls: Click/tap a circle, then click/tap an adjacent circle to swap them.

Scoring:

  • 3 matches: 10 points
  • 4 matches: 25 points + special power-up
  • 5+ matches: 50 points + super power-up
  • Combo multiplier increases with consecutive matches!
